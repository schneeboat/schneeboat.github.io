Have you encountered a long article that you just don’t have the time to read? Here is a trick to help you grasp the main idea. Just by copy-pasting the text, you will see the following:

  • Summary: The most crucial sentence of the article. (Calculated by using vector representations)
  • Wordcloud: Top N most frequently appeared non-trivial words. (NLTK)
  • Sentiment: The decomposed sentiment scores. Whether it has positive, negative, neutral, or compound sentiment.
